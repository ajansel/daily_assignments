Description

A floured tortilla with cheese and chicken. Further description

Ingredients

  • Floured tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Grilled chicken

Directions

  1. Lay down floured tortilla.
  2. Add a layer of cheese.
  3. Add your preferred amount of chicken.
  4. Fold tortilla in half.
  5. PLace tortilla in quesadilla maker until desired crunchiness
